# Covered Bridge Safety Study and Signage (HB 1824)

Legislation requiring PennDOT to examine covered bridges statewide, identify hazards affecting
motorcyclists and cyclists, and install warning signage accordingly.

- Pennsylvania maintains ~219 covered bridges — the most in the United States.
- Hazards: raised joints, gaps, depressions, loose planks, and other irregularities.
- The bill mandates a comprehensive study followed by targeted warnings at dangerous locations
  to protect vulnerable road users.

**Position signal:** Low-cost, targeted public-safety + heritage measure. Study-then-act
structure; protects vulnerable road users without heavy regulatory burden.
