# Authorizing Air Rifles for Big Game Hunting (HB 1538)

Legislation permitting large-caliber air rifles for big game hunting in Pennsylvania.

- Modern large-caliber air rifles can achieve the velocity and energy required for ethical big
  game hunting and differ substantially from traditional BB guns.
- 29 states currently permit this hunting method.
- Day argues the change benefits sportsmen and women, attracts new hunters, and supports
  conservation, operating with proper legislation and oversight for public safety and
  environmental responsibility — "respecting tradition while embracing innovation."

**Position signal:** Pro-sportsmen, pro-Second-Amendment-adjacent constituency. Modernizes
hunting regulation; frames as conservation-positive and tradition-respecting.
